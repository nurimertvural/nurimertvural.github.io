I am a (third-year) PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto and the Vector Institute. I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Murat Erdogdu.

Previously I was a Master student in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University, under the supervision of Prof. Suleyman S. Kozat. For my undergraduate degree I studied Electrical Engineering at Bogazici University. My undergraduate thesis was supervised by Prof. Hamdi Torun.

My current research interests are high-dimensional statistics, statistical learning theory and stochastic optimization. Previously, I also worked on online learning, bandit problems, and non-convex optimization.

You can check my Google Scholar or Semantic Scholar page to learn more about my research/publications. You can find my (selected) works below.
