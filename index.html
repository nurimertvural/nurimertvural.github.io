I received my B.S. degree with high honors, and graduated as the Salutatorian with a CGPA of 3.97/4.00 from Bogazici University, Turkey in June 2018. I am currently an M.S. student with a CGPA of 4.00/4.00 at the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Bilkent University, under the supervision of Prof. Suleyman S. Kozat.

I have achieved the 110th rank nationwide in the National University Entrance Examinations among 2 million students. During my B.S. studies, I have been awarded the Prime Ministry Outstanding Achievement Fellowship. During my M.S. studies, I have been awarded the SPIE Travel Grant (with the maximum amount), Huawei Graduate Fellowship, 5G and Beyond Graduate Research Support and TUBITAK Scholarship for M.S. studies. Additionally, I received 5 Golden Medals in Shahzade Mehmet College Mathematics Competitions and graduated from Halil Kale Science High School as the 3rd in class.

I have authored 7 journal papers in highly respected Transactions, including 6 IEEE Transactions, and 4 conference papers in refereed high impact conference proceedings. My research interests include machine learning, convex/non-convex optimization, online learning, dynamic systems theory, information theory, decision theory, adaptive filtering, statistical signal processing, big data, and computer vision.
